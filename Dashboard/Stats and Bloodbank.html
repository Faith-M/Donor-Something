Fun Fact!..
TheSouth African Blood Servise (SANBS)collects more than 1 000 000 units of blood a year. Each unit of blood measures a little less than half and it is processed to produce various blood products. Every unit of blood is estimated to benefit three individuals and SANBS aims to collect 3 000 units each day.
